About Us

The creation of this application was stemmed from personal experiences of the project team members. Most of us have experienced difficulty in searching for jobs and also an employer searching for the right talent.

JobSeeker was created to serve as a bridge that connects an employer with job opportunities to a job seeker searching for these opportunities
